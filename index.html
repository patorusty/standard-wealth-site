About Standard Wealth

Standard Wealth was born out of my desire to steward my family’s capital in a focused and deliberate strategy. The responsibility of growing the family legacy for the fifth generation was clear to me early on in my formative years as I had the opportunity to be surrounded by exceptional leaders across various business units and most importantly, a precedent of a strong entrepreneurial spirit within the family group of companies.

At Accelerate, we have created a comprehensive investment platform tailored for clients who are focused on generational wealth creation and stewardship plans. Our commitment to fostering long-term relationships is built on the trust and reputation I have curated, which I am proud to stand behind every single day.
